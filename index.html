Coach Thornton's Last Season

After 25 years, Coach Thornton announced his retirement at the end-of-season banquet. There wasn't a dry eye in the room. He had built more than a swim program - he built a family. His final words to us: "Swimming fast is temporary. The bonds you've forged here are permanent. Take care of each other." To this day, the '93 squad still meets every five years...

1994

Ivy League Championships Victory

MK
Mike Kennedy '95 Team Captain, 1994-95

The moment we clinched the title. Coach Johnson's face said it all - years of 5am practices had finally paid off.

This photo was taken right after the final relay. We knew going in that we needed to win it to take the overall championship. The pressure was immense, but somehow that made us swim faster.

I still remember the sound in that natatorium - the echoes, the cheering, the splash of the finish. It's frozen in my memory forever.
